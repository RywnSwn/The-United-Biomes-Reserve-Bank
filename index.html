Diamond Secure™ Checkbook/Credit System

Signed books as checks — ledger-backed and fraud-proof

Checks are most useful for large transfers. Any check not recorded is void.
1

Deposit your diamonds

Hand diamonds to the bank in person. Amount is logged in the UBR ledger under your name and acts as your balance.

2

Transaction

Write the diamond value you're transferring inside the book and sign it. Hand it to the seller as payment. Note that all payments without signiture are invalid.

3

The Seller

The seller will go to a UBR bank branch and hand the check to the bank.

4

Bank Verification

The bank will now take the ammount from the account owner and release it to the seller.

!

Insufficient balance

Incase if your account can't cover the check, Asset Recovery Protocol is triggered. See Enforcement page to see our protocols.

How loans work

1

Request a loan

Agree on the amount and repayment date with the bank.

2

Banker issues diamonds + debt book

You get the diamonds. A signed debt book is created listing amount borrowed (principal), interest rate, and due date. Bank keeps a copy, you keep a copy.

3

Repay on time

Return diamonds to the bank. Both copies of the debt book are voided. Debt cleared.

!

Miss the deadline

Interest keeps compounding. Debt book becomes a warrant. Enforcement Protocol activated.

Asset Recovery Protocol

Official policy regarding delinquent accounts and bad debt

Authorized Enforcement Division

The UBR reserves the right to employ third-party "Recovery Agents" (Enforcers) to settle debts. By using UBR services, you waive your right to inventory protection in the event of this protocol.

1

Notice of Default

Upon the expiration of a loan term or the bouncing of a check, the account is flagged. A 24-hour "Grace Period" is granted unless otherwise specified.

2

Warrant Issuance

If payment is not received, the Ledger entry is converted into a physical Warrant of Seizure. This book contains your last known coordinates and debt total.

3

Liquidation Event

Authorized Enforcers are permitted to use lethal force. All dropped items (armor, tools, resources) are considered UBR property until the debt + 10% processing fee is satisfied.

Master Financial Services Agreement

UBR FORM 10-B | AMENDED APRIL 2024

1. THE PARTIES

This agreement is entered into between the United Biomes Reserve (The Bank) and the Account Holder (The Debtor). By depositing one (1) or more diamonds, the Debtor agrees to all subsequent clauses.

2. LOAN COMPOUND & REPAYMENT

Loans aquire interest at a rate of 5% daily, compounding at the deposit time. Repayment must be made in physical Diamond form. Partial payments do not halt the compounding of the remaining principal.

3. ASSET RECOVERY CLAUSE

In the event of total insolvency (the Debtor having 0 assets), the Bank reserves the right to "Recover Assets." This may include the deconstruction of bases, seizure of villager trading halls, or permanent bounty status until the debt is considered "Paid in Effort."

4. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY

The UBR is not responsible for diamonds lost due to: Lava accidents, Creeper explosions, unauthorized account access, or Server Bugs. All deposits are held at the risk of the Overworld.

5. GOVERNING LAW

This contract is governed by the laws of the Overworld. Disputes shall be settled by in court, or by PVP. All actions will be at the discretion of Ryan.
